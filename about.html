City Grub is a three-day food festival partnering alongside the San Francisco Marin Foodbank. The festival will be hosting a variety of events that take place throughout the day.

Our mission is to bring San Francisco college students and young adults together to increase collective food security.

Food insecurity is a nationwide issue that is hugely present amongst college students. The San Francisco Marin Foodbank's College Pantry program and their various programs provide fresh produce and groceries for eligible college students.

The program is designed so that college students are able to properly pursue their education without worrying about their next meal. From 2019-2020, the San Francisco Marin Foodbank served 995 college students per week.
